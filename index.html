Welcome to the official website of Saswata Bag. Our mission is to design and engineer innovative solutions that combine safety, accessibility, and real-world impact. From developing humane safety systems to creating assistive mobility devices, we focus on taking ideas from concept to prototype and into practical application. This platform showcases our achievements, research projects, and collaborations, while also highlighting our vision of building technology that serves society with responsibility and creativity.
