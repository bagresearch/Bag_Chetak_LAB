Guiram Bag

My Grandfather

Born: 1940 | Passed Away: 2017

Guiram Bag was a dedicated High School teacher, known for his enthusiasm, kindness, and deep commitment to education. Teaching was not just his profession, but his purpose.

Even after retirement, he continued to serve society by teaching and helping underprivileged students, believing that knowledge should reach everyone, regardless of their background.

Writing was his lifelong passion. He authored several books, through which he shared his thoughts, experiences, and observations. His well-known book named Japan Bhramoner iti Britto – জাপান ভ্রমণের ইতিবৃত্ত.

He deeply loved traveling and exploring the world. Every journey inspired him to learn more, observe life closely, and write about the people, cultures, and ideas he encountered.
